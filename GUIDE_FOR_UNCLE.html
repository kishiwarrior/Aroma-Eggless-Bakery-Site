3. How to Add New Products

Steps:

  • Open the Google Sheet and go to the Products tab
  • Click on the first empty row after the last product
  • Fill in the details:
    • id: Create a unique ID (e.g., CAKE-002, BREAD-005)
    • name: Enter the product name
    • category: Choose a category (Cakes, Breads, Pastries, etc.)
    • description: Short description
    • image_url: (Leave empty for now, or add image link)
    • Prices: Enter prices for available sizes
    • Checkboxes: Mark TRUE for relevant categories
    • available: Mark TRUE to show on website
  • Press Enter and you're done! Changes appear on website within 10 minutes.
‚è±Ô∏è Update Speed: After you add or edit a product, it takes up to 10 minutes for changes to appear on the website (due to caching).

6. Tips & Best Practices

‚úÖ DO:

  • Use clear, descriptive product names
  • Fill in all price fields (even if empty, mark them clearly)
  • Use TRUE/FALSE for checkboxes (not Yes/No or other text)
  • Mark products as available = FALSE if they're out of stock
  • Keep category names consistent (e.g., always "Cakes", not "cake" or "CAKES")
  • Test your changes by refreshing the website after 10 minutes

‚ùå DON'T:

  • Delete column headers (they're needed by the website)
  • Leave the "id" field empty (each product needs a unique ID)
  • Use special characters in column names
  • Change the "Products" tab name (the website looks for it)
  • Use Yes/No/Maybe instead of TRUE/FALSE

7. Troubleshooting

‚ùì My product doesn't appear on the website

  • Check if available = TRUE
  • Wait 10 minutes (caching delay)
  • Refresh the website (hard refresh: Ctrl+Shift+R)
  • Check that the product is in the Products tab (not another tab)

‚ùì A product appears twice or in wrong category

  • Check if you accidentally added it twice
  • Verify the category and TRUE/FALSE columns are correct
  • Wait 10 minutes for cache to clear

‚ùì Prices look weird on the website

  • Make sure prices are numbers, not text
  • Don't include ‚Çπ or other symbols in price fields
  • Example: Enter 450, not ‚Çπ450 or Rs. 450

‚ùì I added a new category but don't see the filter

  • Contact Ankit - he needs to add the filter button to the website
  • You can still mark products as TRUE/FALSE in the new column
